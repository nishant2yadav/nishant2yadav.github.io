About Me

PhD in Computer Science at UMass Amherst.

I am a PhD student in Computer Science advised by Prof. Andrew McCallum.  and a member of Information Extraction and Synthesis Laboratory. I am broadly interested in natural language processing and machine learning. A bit more specifically, I am interested in representation learning methods, structured prediction models, clustering, nearest neighbor search and information retrieval.

I received my bachelors in Computer Science from IIT Delhi where I worked with Prof. Amitabha Bagchi and Prof. Subodh Vishnu Sharma. In the past, I was a research intern at Amazon Search, Berkley and Adobe Big Data Experience Labs, Bangalore.
